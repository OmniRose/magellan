Magellan

From validating the rules of composition for latitudes and longitudes to deciding on the format of the output, working with geo-coordinates can be a pretty big pain. Until Magellan, that is. Now, working with coordinates can be as simple as:

$ // decimal-degrees to degrees-minutes-seconds
$ magellan(12.3456).latitude().toDMS() // 12째 20' 44.1594" N
$ magellan(-12.3456).latitude().toDMS() // 12째 20' 44.1594" S
$ magellan(123.456).longitude().toDMS() // 123째 27' 21.6" E
$ magellan(-123.456).longitude().toDMS() // 123째 27' 21.6" W
